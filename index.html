⛓ Ascend — Controls

A roguelike descent to recover the JAM. You act one turn at a time — foes move when you do. Bump into things to act: walk into a foe to attack, a door to open it, a boulder to shove it, your hound or a partner to swap places. On mobile, every key below is on the on-screen pad — tap ⌨ more for the full deck.
