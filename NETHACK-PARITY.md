# Ascend ↔ NetHack Feature Parity & Roadmap

A working map from **canonical NetHack** ([NetHack/NetHack @ `NetHack-5.0`](https://github.com/NetHack/NetHack/tree/NetHack-5.0/src),
133 source files) to **Ascend**, the ASCII roguelike on Polkadot. Each NetHack
`src/*.c` file is roughly one subsystem; we inventory every one, mark Ascend's
status, and plan the missing parts in the Polkadot/web3 idiom.

**Status legend:** ✅ implemented · 🟡 partial · ❌ missing · ⤬ intentionally adapted/cut

> This is a living design doc and the source of truth for the post-Phase-5
> roadmap. Implement top-down; keep the homage faithful but always in the idiom of
> **privacy, independence, resiliency, JAM, and Gavin Wood**.

> **CURRENT STATUS — Phases 6–18 shipped.** The character sheet (attrs/XP/d20/4
> archetypes), deep items (corpses+afflictions, 7 armor slots, erosion, the ray/zap
> engine, tools, blindness/telepathy), magic & faith (energy+extrinsics, Fortune,
> sacrifice, self-fork, ethos/alignment/crowning), the branched dungeon (faucets,
> thrones, chests, lockable doors, the Mempool big room, search/disarm, boulders),
> a growing bestiary, the **full verb set** (Phase 11 ✅), the **full endgame arc**
> (relics → Invocation → the Gehennom maze → Moloch + the JAM → the Censor's hunt →
> the Planes → the aligned Astral offer → ascension → a soulbound deed, Phase 12 ✅),
> conducts + `#audit` + per-archetype Quests (Phase 13), expanded layout generators
> (Phase 14), **level persistence** (Phase 15), a **real branch graph + the Mines +
> the Consensus Vault/Sokoban** (Phase 16), a **fortress + concentric generators +
> per-Plane layouts** (Phase 17), and a **d1–20 balance pass** (Phase 18). Co-op is
> now **peer-authoritative deterministic lockstep** with independent floors, per-player
> logs, signal chat, and an action queue (see §4). The remaining ❌/🟡 below are the
> genuinely-unbuilt parts (Vlad's Tower, vault+guard economy, light/darkness model,
> richer spell/ring/wand/monster breadth, the oracle level, sinks).

## 1. Subsystem inventory (by NetHack source file)

### A. Character & attributes
- **`attrib.c`, `u_init.c`, `role.c`, `exper.c`** — STR/DEX/CON/INT/WIS/CHA, role/race/gender/alignment, XP levels, attribute gain/loss, exercise. → **❌ Ascend has no attributes, role, race, alignment, or XP.** Today the player is a single fixed avatar (20 HP, fixed damage). **Plan (Phase 6):** add the six attributes (Polkadot-flavored display: STR=Stake-weight, DEX=Latency, CON=Resilience, INT=Throughput, WIS=Insight, CHA=Reputation), an XP/level curve (levels = "epochs"), and per-archetype starting kits/stats. Attributes feed to-hit, AC, carry capacity, spell success, prayer.
- **`attrib.c` intrinsics** — speed, see invisible, fire/cold/shock/poison/sleep/disint resistance, telepathy, teleport(itis/control), searching, warning, etc. → **❌ none** (we have ad-hoc ring effects). **Plan (Phase 8):** an intrinsics bitset on the player, gained from corpses (eat → intrinsic), rings, and XP; flavored as "protocol upgrades you've internalized."
- **`insight.c`** (enlightenment/conduct dump) → **❌**. **Plan:** an "audit report" (`#audit`) listing your attributes, intrinsics, vows, and on-chain stats.

Still ❌: Vlad's Tower, morgue/barracks/swamp special rooms, full hand-built special-level scripting.
- **`mkroom.c` special rooms** — shop, temple, throne room, zoo, morgue, beehive, barracks, swamp, leprechaun hall, anthole, cockatrice nest. → **🟡.** Shipped: **shops** (the Marketmaker), a **temple** (an altar tended by a peaceful Gavin **priest**), a **zoo** (a packed menagerie guarding loot), a **treasury vault** (a dense loot room with a locked chest at its heart), and a **morgue** (rotting corpses of dead chains stalked by wraiths) — placed via `placeSpecialRoom`. ## 2. Phased roadmap (post-Phase-5)

Each phase is independently shippable through the existing build → deploy → verify-live loop. Order maximizes "feels like NetHack" per unit of work.

- **Phase 6 — The character sheet.** 🟡 *v1 shipped:* six attributes (STR/DEX/CON/INT/WIS/CHA, flavored), XP/levels ("epochs", max-HP growth on level-up), a **d20 to-hit layer** (level + DEX + enchant vs dodge; STR adds melee damage; misses now happen), a **character sheet** (`@`), and four **archetypes** (Validator, Nominator, Cypherpunk, Builder) with distinct stats + starting kits, pickable in the top bar. *Still to do:* weight + encumbrance, attribute gain/loss & exercise, AC→NetHack evasion model, more archetypes/races.
- **Phase 7 — Items, deep.** 🟡 *7a + 7b shipped.* **7a:** **corpses** drop on kill (eat in place with `e`) → nutrition + **intrinsics** (poison resistance, intrinsic speed) or danger; the **affliction/timeout engine** — a **freezer** (cockatrice) corpse starts **stoning** (`Ston`, deadly countdown), rotten corpses cause **illness** (`Ill`), both cured by prayer/cleanse. **7b:** **seven armor slots** (shirt/body/cloak/helm/gloves/boots/shield — wear one of each); **AC is now evasion** (NetHack-faithful: armor makes you harder to hit, feeding the d20 layer — no more flat soak); **erosion** — a rust bug's touch corrodes worn pieces (rusty→badly corroded), cutting their AC, repaired by prayer and rust-**proofed** ("audited") by a blessed scroll of formal verification; `T` now picks which worn piece to remove. **7d:** the **ray/zap engine** — a **wand of immolation** fires a **bouncing fire ray** that sears every monster (and *you*, if it caroms back off a wall) in its path; plus a wider wand list: **stasis** (sleep a foe), **forking** (polymorph a monster into a random other one), **nullification** (cancel a foe's special powers — splits/steal/ranged/poison/corrode), and **state-read** (probe HP + traits). **7c:** a **tools** class + the **`apply`** (`a`) command — an **excavator** (reusable pick-axe: dig walls in a direction), an **auditor's horn** (unicorn horn: clears poison/confuse/stoning/illness + a small mend), a **contract deployer** (magic marker: write/deploy a scroll from a menu, spending its gas/charges), and a **state reader** (stethoscope: probe an adjacent foe's HP + traits). **7e (blindness):** a **potion of obfuscation** blinds — quaff it and your FOV shrinks to arm's reach (you grope, and swing with a to-hit penalty); throw it to **blind a monster** (it can't find you and wanders). **Telepathy** (eat a censor-imp corpse) lets you *sense every monster's mind while blind*. Cured by prayer, the auditor's horn, a scroll of cleansing, or time. *Still to do (7e cont.):* lit vs. dark rooms + light sources (lamp); containers (multisig vault, chest); detection family; amulets (life-saving, reflection); crystal ball/leash; more wands (wishing, death, striking).
- **Phase 8 — Magic & faith.** 🟡 *8a shipped:* **energy (Pw)** (from INT + epoch, regenerates), **spellbooks** ("runtimes", `+`) studied with `r` (INT-gated, retryable), a **cast** command (`Z`) with an energy + success roll, and six starter **extrinsics** — finality bolt (attack), self-mend (heal), light client (magic map), sense minds (detect monsters), XCM jump (teleport), overclock (haste); Builder/Cypherpunk start knowing one. **8c (faith v1):** **Fortune (Luck)**, −13..+13, now sways every to-hit and dodge; **sacrifice** (`O`) — burn a corpse on (or beside) a Gavin altar for the Architect's favor (Fortune up, a chance of a blessed gift or a mend; rotten offerings displease); the **HODL stone** — a blessed one is a luckstone (+Fortune), a cursed one a **loadstone** you can't drop (BUC hidden until you check); prayer also steadies negative Fortune. **8b (polymorph self):** a **scroll of hard fork** (or a **fork trap**) transforms *you* into a random monster form — you take its **glyph, attacks, speed, and HP pool** for ~20–40 turns, then snap back to your true HP. A fork "dying" isn't death: you collapse back to your saved form and may survive (so polying into a tanky whale is a real escape). HUD shows `Fork:<name> <turns>`. **8d (alignment & crowning):** **ethos** (Order / Balance / Chaos, by archetype), an alignment **favor** record raised by prayer and altar offerings, **godly wrath** (praying before the cooldown lapses now smites you, curses a worn item, or sours your Fortune), and **Fellowship induction** (crowning) — at high favor Gavin inducts you into the **Technical Fellowship** (rank **Architect / Fellow / Adept** by ethos), granting a title, +Fortune, an intrinsic, and a blessed gift. Sets up the Phase-12 Genesis-Plane offering. *Still to do:* lycanthropy (involuntary were-forms); handless-form item restrictions; more spells/schools; deeper intrinsics; per-prayer trouble-priority resolution.
- **Phase 9 — The dungeon, branched.** 🟡 *9a shipped:* **faucets** (`{`, testnet fountains — `q` to quaff for a random boon/bane: heal, a faucet bot, poison, a HODL stone, or it runs dry) and **thrones** (`\`, the Sudo Throne — `s` to sit for full-heal+Fortune, a curse, teleport, pack identify, a level, a guardian, or it crumbles), placed per level. **9b:** **chests** (`(`) placed per level — `o` to open (locked ones must be forced); the loot spills onto the floor (1–3 items, sometimes a HODL stone). **Peaceful-NPC displacement** — walking into a peaceful monster (the Marketmaker) or your nominator now **swaps places** instead of attacking (NetHack-style), so you can step past the shopkeeper; you only fight it once provoked (by shoplifting). **9c:** **lockable doors** — generated doors are now open (`'`), **closed** (`+`, blocks sight + movement; walk into it to push it open), or **locked** (orange `+`; walk into it to **kick** it — STR decides if it bursts, and you can stub your foot). Closed doors hide rooms and wall monsters out until you open them. **9d (special level):** a level-kind hook + **THE MEMPOOL** — NetHack's Big Room, one vast open chamber (a +12-monster swarm arena with scattered loot) at depth 5. **9f (search/disarm/hidden):** `s` now **searches** the adjacent tiles — Insight (WIS) reveals **hidden traps** and **hidden doors** (`#`-disguised, ~8% of doors), and you **disarm** (DEX) any revealed trap beside you — so a path-blocking **reorg trap** is no longer a dead end: step up, `s` to disarm, walk on. **9g (boulders):** pushable **boulders** (`0`) — walk into one to shove it a tile (if the space beyond is clear); they block movement, bolts, *and* monsters (push one into a corridor to wall off pursuers), and a **fire ray** shatters a stuck one. Placed only in open rooms (≥3 open neighbours) so they can never seal a path — the Sokoban foundation. *Still to do:* a branch graph; **the Consensus Vault** (a hand-built Sokoban puzzle using these boulders); **the Mempool Mines** (a real branch); sinks/temples/vaults; `#force` with a weapon + lock-pick tool; expanded trap table; the shop **bill ledger**.

## 4. Where Ascend already *exceeds* NetHack (keep leaning in)

- **On-chain economy** — PAS is a real testnet asset; shops are real wallet transactions.
- **Tradeable NFT relics** — `forge` mints artifacts as ERC-721s with on-chain rarity rolls; they persist across runs and trade on any marketplace. (NetHack artifacts are local save state.)
- **On-chain Hall of Fame + bones** — your deaths seed *other players'* dungeons, permanently, trustlessly.
- **Live P2P co-op** — two adventurers in one shared dungeon (NetHack is single-player), now **peer-authoritative deterministic lockstep** over a serverless WebRTC link: a shared seed + broadcast-on-consume keeps both in sync with no host "seat", each client rendering locally. **Independent floors** (each adventurer fully simulated on its own floor), per-adventurer logs, **signal chat** (distance/LOS-degraded, banner over the map), a **queued-action** badge with LIFO undo, plus friendly-fire and race modes — none of which has a NetHack analog.
- **Procedural adaptive score** — a zero-asset Web-Audio engine: an ambient bed + a rhythm groove that ebbs to ambience when calm, distortion that tracks the *visible threat* around you, and a melodic, BPM-locked combat-tension layer (trills + drums + bass) grown from each zone's own key. NetHack is silent.
- **Mobile** — a touch control deck; NetHack is keyboard-bound.

Keep every new NetHack subsystem wired to this spine: if it touches value, make it on-chain; if it's an artifact, make it a tradeable relic; if it's a milestone, etch it in the Hall of Fame.
