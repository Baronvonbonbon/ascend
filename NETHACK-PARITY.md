133 source files) to **Ascend**, the ASCII roguelike on Polkadot. Each NetHack
`src/*.c` file is roughly one subsystem; we inventory every one, mark Ascend's
status, and plan the missing parts in the Polkadot/web3 idiom.

## 1. Subsystem inventory (by NetHack source file)

### A. Character & attributes
- **`attrib.c`, `u_init.c`, `role.c`, `exper.c`** тАФ STR/DEX/CON/INT/WIS/CHA, role/race/gender/alignment, XP levels, attribute gain/loss, exercise. тЖТ **тЭМ Ascend has no attributes, role, race, alignment, or XP.** Today the player is a single fixed avatar (20 HP, fixed damage). **Plan (Phase 6):** add the six attributes (Polkadot-flavored display: STR=Stake-weight, DEX=Latency, CON=Resilience, INT=Throughput, WIS=Insight, CHA=Reputation), an XP/level curve (levels = "epochs"), and per-archetype starting kits/stats. - **`attrib.c` intrinsics** тАФ speed, see invisible, fire/cold/shock/poison/sleep/disint resistance, telepathy, teleport(itis/control), searching, warning, etc. тЖТ **тЬЕ (Phase 7a).** An **intrinsics set** on the player тАФ **poison resistance, intrinsic speed, telepathy, drain resistance, petrify resistance** тАФ gained by **eating corpses** (a freezer/censor-imp/fork-daemon/barrow-wight), from **rings** (searching/warning/poison-resist), and from **crowning**; surfaced in `#audit`. **Elemental resistances** тЬЕ тАФ **fire / cold / shock resistance** (eat a **finality dragon** for fire, a **darkpool kraken** for cold, a **MEV bot** for shock) grant a **full shrug** against the matching wand ray, spell, or dragon breath. - **`insight.c`** (enlightenment/conduct dump) тЖТ **тЬЕ (Phase 13b).** **`#audit`** (`A`) тАФ a one-screen enlightenment dump: identity/ethos/epoch/XP, all six attributes, HP/AC/En/speed/Fortune, weapon-skill ranks, intrinsics, known extrinsics, active afflictions, endgame progress, and vows kept.
